Our Case Studies

We partnered with Burberry as they were midway through a global Finance Transformation programme. Burberry required programme management support with a portfolio of projects across P2P and OTC, across their SAP S4HANA landscape. We worked alongside the programme team and BPO's to create a global delivery plan to deliver a number of projects end to end, including Ariba SLP, Ariba S2C optimisation, Opentext VIM and BCC optimisation, SAP FSCM reconfiguration, Implementation of P Cards with integration to SAP Concur. We also supported them with the sourcing, selection and planning process for a cash and credit management tool.

LNER approached us as a newly incorporated business when the government took back control of the east coast mainline. The finance functions were still being managed by a shared service centre which was part of the joint venture between Stagecoach and Virgin; Virgin Trains East Coast. The requirement was to build a new finance shared service centre at LNER's headquarters, implement an ERP and a P2P tool. We were able to support them in all aspects of the project; working alongside the CFO to design a new organisational structure for the FSSC, providing a business process transformation solution, creating the programme plan and managing the implementation of both systems end-to-end.

Transdev UK approached us as they were embarking on a new ERP implementation of Microsoft D365, as part of a global rollout of the tool. Their goal was to have someone on the ground to plan and manage the programme in UK. We were able to guide them through the System Integrator (SI) selection process, provide programme planning, a business case and manage the whole ERP end-to-end. With the successful implementation of D365 Finance & Operations, Transdev then decided to implement D365 Procurement & Sourcing integrated with an Inventory Management tool to manage their large fleet of buses.

Following a PE buyout, Servelec contacted us as they had legacy ERP and P2P platforms that had run out of support and required an upgrade. Their finance systems processes and system architecture required a refresh to support the new operating model and to enable them to provide more detailed reporting to the PE. Working alongside the CFO, we developed a new systems strategy, project managed the upgrade of their ERP and transformed a number of finance systems processes.
