T3 Consulting is an independent consultancy business, specialising in Finance Transformation & Systems Implementation. We partner with our clients to transform their Finance functions, embed new technologies and build new capabilities.

Our Purpose

T3 Consulting was built to provide independent services to businesses with no obligation to choose a particular software provider or implementation partner. Our focus and meaning is purely to find the best solution for our clients to transform their processes and deliver benefits to the business, whether this means changing business processes or bringing in new technologies, we focus on what works best for you.

Our Vision

We aim to provide robust, strategic solutions to our clients which enrich them as an organisation. The consultancy market is a crowded place and there are a lot of businesses that come and go, we don't want that. We want to build long term relationships which allow us to prioritise our clients needs and maximise the chances of running successful projects.

Our People

Our knowledge of Finance Transformation & Systems Implementation from around the globe enables us to provide you with experienced individuals who can support you every step of the way. Our team is also supplemented by a wide network of trusted associates.
